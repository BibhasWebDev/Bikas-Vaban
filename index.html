About Us

The West Bengal State Council for School Games & Sports (WBSCSGS) was designed with the aim of providing accessible, affordable, effective and reliable sports facilities in all schools of West Bengal.

In the State of West Bengal, WBSCSGS has made special efforts for reaching out to the community at grassroots level. WBSCSGS focuses on affordable, accessible, accountable, effective and quality services to the masses specially to the school childern.
